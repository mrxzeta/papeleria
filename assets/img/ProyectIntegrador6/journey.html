Day 1

You will start the first day by visiting the iconic Eifferl Tower for panoramic views of the city. After lunch, you will tour the Louvre Museum to admire masterpieces such as the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. To round off the day, you'll dine in an elegant restaurant overlooking the Champs Elysées, followed by a romantic stroll along the banks of the Seine.

Day 2

In the morning you will visit the majestic Notre-Dame Cathedral and stroll through the picturesque district of Le Marais. In the afternoon, discover the splendour of the Palace of Versailles with a half-day excursion from Paris. Finish the day with dinner in a traditional Parisian bistro, followed by a visit to the lively Montmartre district and the Sacré-Coeur Basilica.

Day 3

Start the last day exploring the charming Saint-Germain-des-Prés district and visit the Musée d'Orsay to see the impressive collection of Impressionist art. After lunch, stroll through the Luxembourg Gardens and enjoy a relaxing picnic. To round off your trip, you'll dine in an intimate Latin Quarter, followed by an evening cruise along the Seine to admire the illuminated monuments of Paris.
